Our Mission

Kit Kat Alley Rescue located in Hohenwald, TN, a Veteran Operated, non-profit, no-kill volunteer organization serving the Lewis County and surrounding areas of Tennessee. We are dedicated to being the voice for our feline communities as well as our low income cat owners assisting in unwanted feline population by spay and neuter. Furthermore, we serve the feral cat community and hoarding situations involving TNR (trap, neuter, release) We rescue cats in distress without regard to age, breed or ease of placement. Cats with greatest and immediate need take priority.
